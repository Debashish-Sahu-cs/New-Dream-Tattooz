Return & Refund Policy


At New Dream Tattooz studio, we aim to provide you with the best possible experience, whether you are getting a tattoo, joining our training program, or purchasing products from us. Please read our Return & Refund Policy carefully:


  1. Tattoo Services

    • Booking Deposits

      All tattoo appointment deposits are non-refundable, as they secure your slot and cover preparation costs.

    • Rescheduling

      If you wish to reschedule, please inform us at least 48 hours in advance. Your deposit will be carried forward to the new date.

    • No-shows

      Failure to attend your appointment without prior notice will result in a forfeited deposit.


  2. Training & Certification Programs

    • Cancellation within 7 days

      If you cancel your enrollment within 7 days of payment, you are eligible for a 70% refund (30% covers administrative and resource costs).

    • After 7 days

      No refunds will be issued, but you may request to transfer your seat to a later batch or another eligible participant.

    • No-shows

      If you do not attend your scheduled training without notice, fees are non-refundable.


  3. Merchandise / Products (if applicable)

    • Eligibility

      Products can be returned or exchanged within 7 days of delivery, provided they are unused, unopened, and in original packaging.
