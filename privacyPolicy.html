Privacy & Policy


At New Dream Tattooz studio, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the information you provide while using our website, booking our tattoo services, or enrolling in our training programs.


  1. Information We Collect

    We may collect the following types of information: Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, age, and billing details when you book an appointment, submit feedback, or register for training. Technical Information: IP address, browser type, and device details collected automatically through cookies for website improvement. Health & Safety Information: For tattoos, we may request information related to allergies, skin conditions, or medical precautions (only with your consent).

  2. How We Use Your Information

    We use your information to: Schedule appointments and provide tattoo services. Enroll you in training workshops and issue certifications. Respond to inquiries, feedback, and customer support requests. Send important updates, offers, and service reminders (you can opt out anytime). Improve website performance, security, and user experience.

  3. Information Sharing

    We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Information may only be shared: With trusted service providers (e.g., payment gateways, hosting providers). When required by law, regulation, or legal process.

  4. Data Security

    We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

  5. Cookies & Tracking

    Our website may use cookies to enhance user experience. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not work properly.

  6. Your Rights

    You have the right to: Access, update, or delete your personal information. Withdraw consent for data usage. Opt-out of promotional communications.

  7. Third-Party Links

    Our website may contain links to third-party sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites.

  8. Policy Updates

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Last Updated" date.
