Tattoo Aftercare & Prevention


Q1. What should I do immediately after getting a tattoo?

Leave the protective bandage on for the first 2–4 hours. Wash your hands before touching the tattoo. Gently clean the tattoo with mild, fragrance-free soap and lukewarm water.


Q2. Can I expose my fresh tattoo to sunlight?

Avoid direct sunlight for at least 2–3 weeks. UV rays can fade and damage a healing tattoo.


Q3. When can I go swimming or take long baths?

Avoid swimming pools, hot tubs, and long baths for at least 2 weeks. Only take quick showers to prevent infections.


Q4. Should I apply ointments or creams?

Use a tattoo-recommended healing ointment or fragrance-free moisturizer as advised. Avoid petroleum jelly, as it can clog pores.


Q5. Is itching normal while healing?

Yes, itching is part of the healing process. Do not scratch or peel the tattoo. Lightly moisturize instead.


Q6. How long does a tattoo take to heal?

Surface healing takes 2–3 weeks, but deeper layers may take up to 2–3 months.


Tattoo Services & Bookings


Q1. Do you take walk-ins or only appointments?

We prefer appointments to maintain hygiene and preparation, but walk-ins are welcome if slots are free.


Q2. How do I book an appointment?

You can book online through our website’s booking form or contact us directly via phone/email.


Q3. Do you require a deposit?

Yes, a small non-refundable deposit secures your slot and covers design preparation.


Q4. Do you use safe and hygienic equipment?

Absolutely. We use sterilized, single-use needles and maintain international hygiene standards.


Tattoo Training & Certification


Q1. Do I need prior experience to join your tattoo training?

No prior experience is required. We train beginners and advanced learners.


Q2. Do you provide certification?

Yes, upon successful completion of the program, you’ll receive a recognized certification.


Q3. Do you provide tattoo kits during training?

Yes, students are provided with starter kits and practice materials.


Q4. Can I pay in installments for the training fees?

Yes, we provide flexible payment options. Please contact us for details.
